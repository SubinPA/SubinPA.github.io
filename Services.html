My Services

AutoCAD Drafting Services

With proficiency in AutoCAD, I provide precise and detailed 2D and 3D drafting services. From creating architectural plans to engineering drawings, I ensure accuracy and compliance with industry standards, optimizing designs for seamless integration with other disciplines.

Construction Management

As an experienced civil engineer, I excel in construction management services. My hands-on approach includes site supervision, progress monitoring, and quality control, ensuring timely delivery and adherence to safety regulations throughout the construction process.

Revit BIM Modeling

Utilizing Revit's Building Information Modeling (BIM) capabilities, I develop intelligent 3D models that improve collaboration and facilitate efficient project coordination. From clash detection to construction scheduling, my BIM expertise enhances project outcomes.

Site Analysis and Feasibility Studies

Prior to commencing any project, I conduct thorough site analysis and feasibility studies to evaluate the viability and potential challenges of the proposed development. By considering factors such as geotechnical conditions, environmental impact, and regulatory requirements, I ensure that projects are set up for success from the outset. This comprehensive approach helps in making informed decisions and optimizing the overall project outcome.

Structural Analysis and Design

With a focus on structural integrity, I conduct thorough analyses using advanced software. From load calculations to seismic assessments, my designs optimize material usage, resulting in resilient and cost-effective structures.

Sustainable Construction Consultation

As a civil engineer committed to environmental responsibility, I offer sustainable construction consultation services. From green building materials to energy-efficient designs, I help clients minimize their ecological footprint while achieving their project goals.
