About Me

Hello! I am a dedicated and experienced civil engineer with a passion for construction and project management. With a solid background in overseeing various construction projects, I have acquired valuable skills and knowledge that enable me to excel in the field. From January 2020 to December 2020, I had the opportunity to work at Aswathy Gardens Limited, a reputable construction company. During my tenure there, I played a pivotal role in the construction of numerous villas. My responsibilities included overseeing critical aspects such as brickwork, plastering, concreting, and other essential construction tasks. This experience allowed me to gain hands-on knowledge of the construction process and the intricacies involved in delivering high-quality projects. Following my successful stint at Aswathy Gardens Limited, I joined Radianz in January 2021. While working at Radianz, I had the privilege of supervising the construction of a remarkable 20-floor building. This project provided me with an opportunity to further refine my project management skills, as I navigated the complexities of overseeing a large-scale construction endeavor. Continuing my professional growth, I then joined Putribuilders as a civil engineer. Due to my dedication and strong performance, I was soon promoted to the role of Team Lead. As a Team Lead, I efficiently managed a group of talented professionals, fostering a collaborative work environment and ensuring the successful completion of various projects. Throughout my career, I have developed a deep understanding of construction methodologies, industry standards, and regulatory requirements. I am adept at utilizing my technical expertise to solve complex problems, coordinate with diverse stakeholders, and deliver projects within designated timelines and budgets. I thrive in fast-paced environments, and my excellent organizational and communication skills allow me to effectively manage multiple tasks simultaneously. I am a detail-oriented professional, known for my meticulous approach to quality assurance and my commitment to delivering exceptional results. In summary, I am a skilled civil engineer with a solid foundation in construction project management. With my proven track record of successfully overseeing projects and my passion for excellence, I am confident in my ability to contribute significantly to any construction team.
